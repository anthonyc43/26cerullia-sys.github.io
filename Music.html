This year, I started learning how to create sound effects and produce music. In 2024 and 2025, I served as the music producer for two video games called "Fightatron" and "Echo Chambers". Here are pictures of both games and a link to one of the songs I made for Echo Chambers. Music production has become a significant part of my creative outlet, allowing me to blend my love for gaming with artistic expression. I use tools like Soundtrap to compose and edit tracks, and I'm excited to continue developing my skills in audio design.
